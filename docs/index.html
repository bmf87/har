Human Activity Recognition (HAR)

Inertial Dataset
body_acc_x body acceleration signal for x-axis. Body acceleration signals obtained by subtracting gravity from total acceleration
body_acc_y body acceleration signal for y-axis
body_acc_y body acceleration signal for z-axis
body_gyro_x angular velocity for x-axis
body_gyro_y angular velocity for y-axis
body_gyro_z angular velocity for z-axis
Description: dataset is multivariate (tri-axial), time-series data from an accelerometer and gyroscope sampled while volunteer subjects performed one of six activities of daily life (ADL).
